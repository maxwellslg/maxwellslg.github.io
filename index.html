A Maxwellian is someone who:
...worked at a Tasmanian devil conservation sanctuary in Tasmania, Australia.
...knows how to do 6 different kinds of Latin dance.
...was born with a broken leg. Strange, yes. You could say she was just too eager for life!
...peaked in life when she took the SATs in the room next door to Emma Watson, and has been overcompensating ever since. (She wore red sweatpants)
...was a Dukie from the start -- born in the Duke Hospital
...wants to run a marathon on every continent
...can quote the Parent Trap.
...spent months memorizing 400 digits of pi and then lost a pi-memorization competition by 1 number back in 6th grade.
...moved from Pennsylvania to Florida on a school bus that her family bought on eBay.
...hasn’t had french fries in over five years! After a traumatizing experience at a Wendy's, added to that time of year for Lent, what better thing to give up?
...claims to be a sports betting pro
...has moved 50 times--yes, you read that right, FIFTY times.
...auditioned for American Idol over the summer before senior year.
...has successfully collected all 50 state quarters.
...was flown out to Los Angeles with his buddies for winning an Online Comedy Video Contest.
...has a phobia of caterpillars.
...knows how to call ptarmigans (They're the state bird of Alaska).

About Us

While we spend a lot of time hanging out with each other either in section or out, we also host a variety of open and closed events. Each semester, we host 3-4 section parties and a number of house parties and events that are exclusively for members of Maxwell.

Housing Section

Maxwell’s section is in Keohane 4D, connected to McClendon Tower and next door to Keohane 4B. This is where most of our recruitment events will be held, unless otherwise stated. In addition, you’ll find 60% of Maxwell at any point after midnight ordering a haystack in Provisions. Join us!
